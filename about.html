About

You may be wondering why my website is titled "life as lain," when my name is Iain; spelt with an 'I'. One, hyphinated, word: Sans-serif. Every roll sheet seems to use Sans-serif font which leaves a nervous teacher, on the first day of class, with no chance of differentiating a capital 'I' from a lower case 'l'.

I attended the same school most of my life. This got to the point that my friends and classmates would correct the teachers for me (which worked out in my favor, because I have grown tired of making the distinction evident).

This still happens frequently, but I just let it slide, and sometimes, I assume the newly appointed name. At the end of the day, what is a name really?

I am very interesting in these sorts of questions and how they pertain to Comp Sci; the notion of Abstraction is one I find very interesting (maybe I will write a blog about it).

My goal in this site is to give a you an opportunity to learn about me in a way that is not boring. (e. g. check out this little binary addition quiz i wrote in PHP)
